Your Aspiration for a prosperous Tamil Nadu

Ungal Viruppam Engal Arikkai

Bharatiya Janata Party, under the able guidance of Honourable Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, aims to make Tamil Nadu the pinnacle of development.

Tamil Nadu has always shown India the way ahead in every sector be it finance, telecommunications, research and development or preserving traditional values thereby acting as trailblazers in all walks of life.

It's time that Tamil Nadu outperform at the international platform as well.

Bharatiya Janata Party, Tamil Nadu has launched the “Ungal Viruppam Engal Arikkai'' campaign to chart out a road map for the long-term development of Tamil Nadu, after consulting with the people of the state.

We invite you to become a part of this campaign and share your vision for Tamil Nadu
